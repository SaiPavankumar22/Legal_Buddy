Why this project exists

People regularly accept terms, privacy policies, and contract clauses they do not fully understand. Legal Buddy turns local Gemma inference into a safety layer.

This project is fully local-first at the model layer. The backend talks to Ollama on localhost and uses Gemma as the reasoning engine for both legal Q&A and multimodal document analysis.

The Indian-law corpus comes from the bundled `legal_texts.npy` and `legal_faiss.index` files, which makes the legal chat more grounded than a generic chatbot.

Why `gemma4:e2b`

A practical local model for privacy-sensitive user documents and lightweight hackathon demos.

  • Runs through Ollama on localhost, keeping the product local-first.
  • Handles text chat and image-based document inspection in one workflow.
  • Lets you show judges intentional model selection around privacy, accessibility, and deployment simplicity.

Suggested demo flow

Use these three steps in the hackathon presentation.

  1. Ask a grounded legal question about tenancy, fraud, salary withholding, or consumer rights.
  2. Upload a privacy policy PDF or screenshot-heavy terms page and let Gemma highlight risky clauses.
  3. Explain that the app stays local around Ollama and uses the Indian legal corpus for better relevance.
